Mihrako Hotel & Spa
Salim Street 10, 46001 As Sulaymānīyah, Iraq
Reserve
Featuring free WiFi and a sauna, Mihrako Hotel & Spa offers accommodation in As Sulaymānīyah. Guests can enjoy the on-site restaurant.
All rooms are fitted with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Some units feature a seating area to relax in after a busy day. All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom fitted with a shower. Extras include slippers and a hair dryer. Free private parking is also available at this hotel. A TV is provided.

Mihrako Hotel and Spa has a spa with free access to all guests.

You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property.

Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 8.9 for a two-person trip.
Mihrako Hotel & Spa has been welcoming hotel.com guests since 6 Aug 2015.
